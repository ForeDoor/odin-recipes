Spinach Pizza

Description

Sun dried tomatoes, spinach, and Kalamata olives boldly flavor this easy pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked Italian pizza crust
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 small red onion, halved and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil from the sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Adjust oven rack to lowest position, and heat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, garlic and 1/2 cup feta in a small bowl.
  3. Place pizza crust on a cookie sheet.
  4. Spread mayonnaise mixture over pizza crust, then top with tomatoes, olives and oregano.
  5. Bake until heated through and crisp, about 10 minutes.
  6. Toss spinach and onion with the 1 Tb. sun-dried tomato oil.
  7. Top hot pizza with spinach mixture and remaining 1/2 cup feta cheese.
  8. Return to oven and bake until cheese melts, about 2 minutes longer.
  9. Cut into 6 slices and serve.
